About Me

Fullstack Javascript Engineer - my journey.

Thanks for checking this out, my new website is coming soon! This was a site I created to demonstrate how a vanilla JavaScript static site with CSS3 and HTML5 could be responsive without Frameworks or Libraries. I graduated from a Comp Sci Degree in 1995, coding back then was a different experience. That early coding with functional programming theory helped get me back into coding when I returned many years later as a MERN/MEAN Javascript Node developer.

I managed Unix and Oracle SAP systems for 10 years, and following this I created sites for local businesses freelance using CSS HTML and Javascript. I wrapped up my web dev business to move to the US in 2016 but my interest was tweaked in creating apps, and back into full stack coding I went. I completed a UNH 6 month in class bootcamp in 2016-2017 based here in Portsmouth NH, to meet other coders, find a community, and level up. Here is My Resume

My freelance company Deewhy Consultants LLC has brought me from Sydney in Auzzie to Dungarvan, Ireland and now Portsmouth NH. I am obsessed with coding, and am competent in front or backend. CSS, HTML, Javascript, React, Angular, Vue, Node JS, mySQL, MongoDB, AWS, Github, Bitbucket, Jira, Trello. I enjoy data visualization, unit testing, and also working closely with clients making their ideas visable online. Tweet to @louhayes3
