About Me

What I'd Like You To Know:

I bring a lot of different hats to the table. My Resume is extensive. I obtained my Computer Science Degree 20 years ago, in Galway RTC, Ireland. After 2 year Cobol programmer role I landed in SAP. I spent 7 years working in large ERP companies installing and upgrading SAP systems on multiple hardware, OS and DB platforms.

In 2008, I started my own Web site develope and hosting company with my programmer partner. Web Developer of medium to large projects, static and e-commerce sites, client training, wireframes, CSS, HTML, Javascript, Node JS, mySQL, Joomla, Wordpress, Typo3, Photoshop, AWS, Github, Trello. Worked closely with clients.

In 2016 I moved to Portsmouth NH in the US and have continued to learn latest frameworks by taking part in a UNH Bootcamp Fullstack Course. I am currently working with Mobile Programming LLC, creating Angular5 SVG Chart App and have created Apps in React for local schools (my favourite is a Watson IBM Speech to Text reading Lab).
